Easy Fried Rice

Description

This simple fried rice recipe is a great way to use leftover rice and turn it into a delicious meal in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set them aside.
  3. Saut√© carrots and peas until soft.
  4. Add rice, green onions, and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  6. Return eggs to the pan, mix, and cook for another 2 minutes.
