Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is rich, cheesy, and layered with savory beef and tomato sauce. Perfect for a comforting family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  3. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake covered for 25 minutes, then uncovered for 15 minutes.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
