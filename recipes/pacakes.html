Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are quick and easy to make. Perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk together dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (don't overmix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook the other side until golden.
